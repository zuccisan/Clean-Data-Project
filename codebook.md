Identifiers
subject - The ID of one out of 30 volunteers within an age bracket of 19-48 years. 
activity - The type of activity performed. There are six activities (WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING)
